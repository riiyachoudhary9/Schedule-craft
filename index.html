Everything you need to plan your week

Two timetable types, two generation modes, and five export formats.

Academic & Personal

Choose an academic timetable for classes and labs, or a personal one for routines, work and habits.

Random Generator

Feed in your subjects and days — ScheduleCraft shuffles them into a balanced, ready-to-use timetable.

Build Your Own

Prefer full control? Manually place each subject into the exact slot you want.

5 Export Formats

Download your timetable as CSV, Excel, PDF, PNG or JPEG with a single click.

History & Downloads

Every timetable you create is saved so you can revisit, re-export or delete it later.

Personal Accounts

Sign up to keep your timetables organised under your own account.
